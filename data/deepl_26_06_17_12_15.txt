I hereby declare the session of the European Parliament, which was suspended on Friday 17 December, to be resumed. I would like to wish you all the best once again for the New Year and hope that you had a lovely holiday.
As you will have noticed, the much-feared ‘millennium bug’ has not materialised. However, citizens of some of our Member States have fallen victim to terrible natural disasters.
There is a desire within Parliament to hold a debate during this part-session in the coming days.
